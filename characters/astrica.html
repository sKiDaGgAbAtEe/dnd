Class & Race Features
Arcane Recovery 1/Day · Short Rest ▶
Once per day on a Short Rest: recover spell slots totaling ≤ half wizard level (round up). At Level 1: recover one 1st-level slot. Cannot recover 6th level+.
Wizard Spellbook 6 Spells · +2/Level ▶
6 prepared 1st-level spells. Gain 2 spells per level. Copy from scrolls and tomes (2 hrs + 50 gp per spell level). The book smells of old forest and ink.
Witherbloom Initiate Strixhaven Feat ▶
Gain Strixhaven Initiate feat. Witherbloom spells added to wizard list. Proficient with Herbalism Kit.
Darkvision · 60 ft Fairy ▶
Dim light within 60 ft as bright light. Darkness as dim light. Cannot discern color in darkness — only shades of grey.
Fairy Dust 1/Short Rest · Bonus Action ▶
Bonus action: resize non-magical equipment to fit your body (or return to normal). Once per short rest. Permanent on non-magical items.
Fey Ancestry Passive · Fairy ▶
Advantage on saves vs. charmed. Magic cannot put you to sleep. People have tried. It doesn't work.
Hidden Step 2 Uses · Long Rest ▶
Bonus action: become invisible until start of your next turn, or until you attack, deal damage, or force a save. Uses = proficiency bonus (2). Recover on long rest.
Friend of the Forest Passive · Fairy ▶
Communicate with beasts and plants in limited ways. You understand them; they don't understand you. Advantage on CHA checks to influence them.
One With Nature Druidcraft Cantrip ▶
Know and cast Druidcraft at will, using WIS as the casting ability. Whisper to nature: predict weather, create harmless sensory effects, light or snuff small flames. 30 ft range.
Small Hands Big Dreams Fairy ▶
Disadvantage on Athletics (STR) checks. Advantage on Persuasion (CHA) checks. She makes up for every inch she lacks.
Character
Personality Traits ▶
Small in stature, enormous in presence. Her curiosity is insatiable — she will touch everything, ask about everything, remember everything. Her laughter is genuine; her silences are calculating.
Ideals ▶
Knowledge without application is just hoarding. She studies the boundary between life and death not out of morbidity, but because she believes something important lives there — something the world is pretending not to notice.
Bonds ▶
Her spellbook contains pressed flowers, margin notes in three languages, and a spell she hasn't been able to cast yet. The people she travels with have become, unexpectedly, something she would cross swamps for.
Flaws ▶
She occasionally forgets other people cannot fly, hide instantly, or talk to mushrooms — leading to plans that only work if you're her. She also underestimates her own size in physical situations.
