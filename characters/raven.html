Features & Traits
Sneak Attack1d6 · Once/Turn▶
Extra 1d6 damage once per turn with finesse or ranged weapon when you have advantage, or an ally is within 5 ft of target. No disadvantage required. She doesn't explain how she does it. You just notice the blood.
Thieves' CantPassive · Hidden Language▶
Secret criminal language embedded in normal speech. She uses it without thinking. "The message sounds like nothing. It's never nothing."
Expertise ✦ ×2Stealth +7 · SoH +7▶
Double proficiency on Stealth and Sleight of Hand. DEX +3, Prof ×2 = +7 each. She does not make noise. Objects do not stay where you put them. These are not skills. These are instincts.
Blessing of the Raven Queen1/SR · Bonus Action▶
Teleport up to 30 ft to a visible unoccupied space. Become incorporeal until start of your next turn — resistance to all damage except force. The shadow swallows her. She steps out elsewhere. She finds this deeply satisfying.
Necrotic ResistancePassive · Shadar-Kai▶
Resistance to necrotic damage — half damage from all necrotic sources. The Shadowfell is familiar. Death is not hostile. They have an understanding.
Darkvision · 60 ftShadar-Kai▶
Dim light as bright light, darkness as dim light within 60 ft. She prefers the grey. Easier to disappear into.
Fey AncestryShadar-Kai · Passive▶
Advantage on saves vs. charmed. Magic cannot induce sleep. She is not easily held by anything she hasn't chosen.
Assassin (Unlocks Lv3)Auto-Crit · Surprise▶
Assassinate: Advantage vs. creatures who haven't acted. Hits against surprised creatures = automatic critical hit.
Bonus Profs: Disguise Kit, Poisoner's Kit.
She's already operating this way. Level 3 makes it official.
Criminal ContactSpy Background▶
Anonymous contact in the criminal underworld. Passes contracts and requests. She is known in certain circles as someone who delivers. Clean finishes. No loose ends.
Shiny Trinket CompulsionThe Secret▶
She pockets things that catch light — rings, beads, polished coins, broken chains. Organized carefully in a shadow-lined pouch. She knows exactly where each one came from. Some are trophies. Some are from moments with her chosen people. She will never confirm this.

"It's tactical."
Character
Personality ▶
Soft voice. Sharp grin. Constant eye contact. Says things she absolutely should not say — then backs them up. She grew up small and overlooked. So she learned to prove people wrong in permanent ways. The smirk doesn't leave. Not even when she's working.
Ideals ▶
"I'm loyal to my friends, but not to any ideals — and everyone else can take a trip down the Styx for all I care." No kingdoms. No causes. Just hers. She doesn't believe in things. She believes in people. Specifically: the small number she has chosen.
Bond ▶
"My ill-gotten gains go to support my family." She takes the contracts. She finishes them clean. The gold moves to people she doesn't name aloud. They don't need to know where it comes from. They just need to be safe. That's the whole arrangement.
Flaw ▶
"If there's a plan, I'll forget it. If I don't forget, I'll ignore it." She trusts instinct over structure. She will break formation, take the shortcut, trigger combat early. She usually makes it work. The key word is usually.
